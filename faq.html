Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know about our sourcing, authentication, and restoration.

Our lead researchers use a combination of provenance tracing, material analysis, and stylistic dating. Every piece priced over $500 comes with a Certificate of Authenticity signed by our lead historian detailing its origin and estimated era.

Absolutely. If a piece in our inventory is in "as-found" condition, we can restore it to your specifications—whether that means a high polish on brass or specific upholstery for seating. Contact us before purchasing to discuss options.

Due to the unique and fragile nature of antiques, all sales are final. We provide incredibly rigorous condition reports, dimensions, and high-resolution images so you can purchase with total confidence.

We work tightly with European estate liquidators, demolition contractors working on highly historic buildings, and private collectors. Our aim is to rescue items just before they are permanently lost.
