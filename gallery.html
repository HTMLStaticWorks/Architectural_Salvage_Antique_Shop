Case Study

The Cotswold Barn Conversion

When an 18th-century barn in Gloucestershire was slated for conversion, the owners turned to Heritage Salvage to help anchor the modern interior with historical weight.

We provided 400 linear feet of reclaimed French Oak beams and a suite of industrial factory pendants. The result is a seamless transition between agrarian history and minimalist luxury.

Style Synthesis

Mixing Eras

Don't be afraid to pair a 1920s Art Deco mirror with a mid-century modern vanity. The key is to find a common thread—be it the warmth of the metal or the geometry of the lines.

Material Balance

If your space is dominated by glass and steel, anchor it with the organic texture of reclaimed wood or the earthy patina of weathered brass to prevent the room from feeling clinical.
