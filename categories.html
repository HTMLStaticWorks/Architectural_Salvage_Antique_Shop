The Preservation Pledge

Every object in our collection represents a fragment of history rescued from the verge of loss. We pledge to honor the original craftsmanship by using only reversible conservation methods and providing full transparency on the provenance of every element we source.

Rescue

Preventing historical materials from entering the waste stream.

Maintaining the authentic patina and character of the eras past.
