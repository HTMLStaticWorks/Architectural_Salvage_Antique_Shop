Our Heritage

Decades of passion for uncovering and preserving architectural history.

The Beginning

Founded in 1998, Salvage & Co. began as a small workshop dedicated to restoring forgotten Victorian doors. Over time, our appreciation for craftsmanship drove us to seek out pieces across Europe, saving irreplaceable heritage from demolition.

We believe that every scuff, patina, and weathering mark tells a story. Our mission is to pass those stories on to you, giving these historical elements a new lease on life in modern homes and commercial spaces.

Our Restoration Process

We preserve the authenticity of every piece. Our master artisans use traditional techniques, ensuring that brass is lightly polished without removing its age, and wood is treated with natural waxes rather than modern varnishes.

  • Sourcing purely from pre-1940s estates.
  • Conservation over heavy restoration.
  • Detailed historical documentation for each piece.
